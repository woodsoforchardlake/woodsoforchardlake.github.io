Welcome, Subdivision Residents!

The Woods of Orchard Lake is a beautiful Luxurious wooded community nestled between Upper Straights Lake, Middle Straights Lake and Green Lake. An enchanting area of rolling land, woods, and water, the streets winding throughout the community and neighborhood are professionally maintained and common areas are manicured giving all who enter the true feeling of being welcome. It is the perfect community for those who value a rich township history.

The opportunities to enjoy a richer more rewarding life are all around you. The quality of life, community aesthetics, solid property values and surroundings are paramount to the community.

The Woods of Orchard Lake is a very special place to call home.
